  • Doom is a video game franchise that first gained popularity in the early 1990s. Its debut title, DOOM(1993) is often considered one of the greatest first generation first person shooter titles and a meme in itself. In 2016, it was rebooted with the DOOM(2016) title, which while once again being an incredibly impressive FPS title, has some heavy colorspace bias. While it was a sound artistic decision, it has spawned at least one meme.
